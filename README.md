## âš–ï¸ Methodological Principles
To ensure rigor and transparency, this research adheres to the following principles:

1.  **Transparency in Uncertainty:** Explicitly stating known unknowns, particularly regarding data gaps in Global South contexts.
2.  **Policy Relevance:** Every theoretical framework includes a "Policy Translation" section mapping concepts to actionable regulatory levers.
3.  **Centered, Not Cited:** Global South perspectives are treated as central theoretical lenses, not merely as case studies for Western frameworks.
4.  **Reproducibility:** Commitment to Open Science principles where data privacy permits.
